PIAIC offers the following courses:

  • Cloud Native Computing
  • Blockchain Computing

Artificial Intelligence

The artificial intelligence programme of the PIAIC takes the students through the foundation of the AI technology, via teaching programming in open source languages like Python, Pandas, Numpy, Tensorflow 2.0 and flask and traching Deploying practicle AI solutions and use cases, and ending with real_life examples of modren day AI in practices.








Cloud Native Computing

The cloud native computing programmes prepare the students to program in open source technologies like Linux, Docker, Kubernets, Javascripts, Node.JS, React and React Native while introducing students to development of Microservices








Blockchain Computing

The main emphasise to understand blockchain computing programe to Understanding like Ethereum and Hyperledger (fabric) and learn to program in language such as Solidity, Javascripts on frameworks like Node.JS.








Internet Of Things

The Internet of Things programe introduces the students to open source development technologies like Rust, Embedded Rust, WebAssembly, Hyper, Google Assistent, and Kubernets While making them efficient in Developing Microservices for IoT Edge, IoT Cloud and Voice Computing For Device Controle.
